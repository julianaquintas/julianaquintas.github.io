Interactive Energy

Overview

Interactive Energy is an installation in the Future Room of Estação Memória, a museum in Fafe, Portugal. The exhibit invites visitors to pedal stationary bikes, generating motion in the wind turbines displayed on a wall projection.

This interactive experience was developed in Unity, using Phidgets to gather input from the bikes. It gamifies the experience of eco-friendly transportation to promote the city’s green initiatives.

My Contributions

  • programmed gameplay mechanics for the interactive wall projection, including a co-op energy-generation system;
  • integrated inputs from Phidget sensors in Unity through the use of the Phidget22 library;
  • developed sound design, including dynamic audio feedback responsive to user input;
  • implemented the responsive UI, wich adapts to the number of players and the selected language.
