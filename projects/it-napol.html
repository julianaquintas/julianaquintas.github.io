Napoleonic Itineraries

Overview

Napoleonic Itineraries is a multi-platform immersive project that recreates events of the Napoleonic Wars that took place in Portugal. This engaging XR approach allows visitors to witness historic moments through VR experiences available in 13 tourism offices. A companion location-based mobile app, available for both Android and iOS, further promotes historical sites: by visiting certain locations, the users unlock the guidance of historical characters through augmented reality.

My Contributions

  • researched historical data to ensure accuracy in character designs, regiment uniforms and event timelines;
  • developed gameplay flowcharts and early script drafts during pre-production for the VR experiences;
  • assisted the 3D and design teams in fact-checking historical content to maintain storytelling consistency;
  • designed and implemented sound design, dialogue integration, and subtitles in four languages (PT, EN, FR, ES) for VR experiences in Unreal Engine and AR experiences in Unity;
