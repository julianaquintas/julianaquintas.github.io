Odd Space VR

Overview

Odd Space VR is an edutainment experience designed to showcase the HP Reverb 2 headset.

Developed in Unity, this virtual reality demo allows the player to explore the inner Solar System and uncover its history across two parallel timelines.

My Contributions

  • implemented gameplay mechanics for collecting pickups and unlocking content;
  • managed unlockable content through Unity Addressables to keep a modular and scalable structure, which supports future expansions;
  • refactored game logic to support dual-timeline functionality with dynamic updates to gameplay and visual elements;
  • developed accessibility features, including an interactive tutorial;
  • enhanced immersion through dynamic sound design.
